Privacy Policy

Dropit — Drop anything. It'll be there when you need it.

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Dropit ("we", "our", "the Service") is a personal archiving tool operated via Telegram Bot (@motg_drop_link_bot) and Telegram Mini App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from Telegram

  • Telegram User ID — to identify your account and separate your data from other users
  • Display name — to personalize the Mini App experience
  • Messages you send to the bot (URLs, text memos, to-dos)

2.2 Information We Generate

  • Metadata extracted from URLs you share (page title, description, thumbnail via OG tags)
  • AI-generated content: category classification and one-line summaries of your saved items

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Phone number
  • Contacts or address book
  • Location data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Save and organize the content you send to the bot
  • Generate AI-powered categorization and summaries
  • Display your archived content in the Telegram Mini App
  • Detect duplicate URLs

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Dropit:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Telegram Bot API Receive and send messages Messages you send to the bot
Supabase Database storage Saved URLs, memos, metadata, user ID
Grok API (xAI) AI categorization & summarization URL metadata and page content

Each third-party service is governed by its own privacy policy. We only share the minimum data necessary for each service to function.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database
  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Access to the database is restricted to the service administrator
  • We do not store your Telegram authentication tokens

6. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as you use the Service
  • You may request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us
  • If you stop using the Service, your data remains stored unless you request deletion

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — View all data we store about you (visible in the Mini App)
  • Delete — Request complete deletion of your data
  • Export — Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Dropit is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
